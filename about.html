About Us

Acropolis Technical Campus (formerly Transnational Knowledge Society Group of Institutions) is a college in India. Formed in the 2009, it is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV) in Bhopal, and Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV) in Indore. Located at Tillore, Indore, Madhya Pradesh, the college offers bachelor of engineering and MBA degrees. It is preceded by the Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research. They have a wi-fi campus.
